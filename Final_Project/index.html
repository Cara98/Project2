My Artwork

My name is Cara Sudler. I attend Parkway Center City. I started drawing since I like was six years old. It started from copying picture from a coloring book to being able to draw on a canvas. When I draw and listen to music, it's my way of getting away from the world. Then I started expanding my art by drawing on people and shoes. My twin sister always let me draw on her, it was like I was giving her a tattoo hilarious right. The first pair of shoes I drew on was my timberlands.

They caught a lot of attend and I was proud of myself. Then I started drawing on my family memebers shoes. So basically i can draw on any and everything. Now people started to ask if i could draw them tattoos, I've thought about being a tattoo artist.
